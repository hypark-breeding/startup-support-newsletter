# Startup Support Newsletter

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
Help agents find Korean government and public-sector startup opportunities, verify official sources, and turn the results into practical briefings, schedules, designed reports, newsletters, and email deliveries.

## Users
Founders, operators, and startup support researchers who need fast, trustworthy summaries without manually checking many public-sector websites.

## Tone
Calm, official, concise, and useful. The interface should feel like a trusted research memo, not a marketing landing page or a metrics dashboard.

## Design Principles
- Prioritize readable email output over decorative layout.
- Keep official source links visible and easy to act on.
- Separate currently actionable items from watchlist items.
- Avoid long cards, dense badges, heavy shadows, dark backgrounds, and ornamental chrome.

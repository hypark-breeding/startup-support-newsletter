# Design Direction

## Style Prompt

A civic intelligence dashboard for Korean startup founders: crisp editorial typography,
paper-like surfaces, decisive calendar grids, and restrained public-service colors. The
interface should feel trustworthy and operational, closer to a policy newsroom desk than
a marketing landing page.

## Colors

- `#F7F4ED` canvas: warm document background.
- `#15231F` ink: primary text.
- `#B33A2F` urgent: deadline and risk emphasis.
- `#1F6F68` verified: official source and active states.
- `#D7C7A2` rule: borders, dividers, and subtle fills.
- `#2E4E7E` info: briefings and neutral schedule events.

## Typography

- Display: `Fraunces`, Georgia, serif.
- Interface: `IBM Plex Sans KR`, `Noto Sans KR`, sans-serif.
- Numerals: `IBM Plex Mono`, monospace.

## Motion

- Calendar cells reveal with short staggered opacity and vertical movement.
- Active event rows use a small left-rule expansion instead of large motion.
- Avoid decorative animation that competes with dates and deadlines.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use generic blue SaaS gradients.
- Do not hide program titles behind cards that require too much clicking.
- Do not make deadlines look like ordinary events.
- Do not use purple-heavy palettes.
- Do not use oversized hero marketing sections.
